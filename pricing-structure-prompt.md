# Pricing Structure Prompt

## Goal
Turn a rough service idea into a clearer and more usable pricing structure.

## Prompt structure
Act as a strategic pricing assistant.

Based on the following service or offer, create a simple pricing structure that is:
- clear
- realistic
- easy to understand
- suitable for presentation on a website or sales message

Organize the output into:
- core offer
- optional extras
- pricing tiers if relevant
- short explanation of the pricing logic

Keep the result practical and aligned with service-based businesses.

## Use case
Useful for freelancers, consultants, service businesses and digital offers that need more structured pricing.
