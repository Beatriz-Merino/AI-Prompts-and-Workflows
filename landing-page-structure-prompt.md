# Landing Page Structure Prompt

## Goal
Turn a rough business idea or service into a clearer landing page structure.

## Prompt structure
Act as a strategic landing page assistant.

Based on the following business idea or service, create a simple landing page structure that is clear, user-friendly and conversion-oriented.

Organize the output into sections such as:
- headline
- subheadline
- problem
- solution
- services or offer
- trust elements
- call to action

Keep the structure practical, concise and suitable for a modern service website.

## Use case
Useful for small businesses, personal brands and service providers that need a clearer website structure.
