# Positioning Prompt

## Goal
Turn a vague business idea into a clearer positioning statement.

## Prompt structure
Act as a strategic positioning assistant.

Based on the following business, offer or project, define:
- who it is for
- what problem it solves
- what makes it different
- what value it provides
- a short positioning summary

Keep the result clear, practical and suitable for use in a website, profile or sales message.

## Use case
Useful for freelancers, service businesses, personal brands and digital projects that need a clearer identity and message.
