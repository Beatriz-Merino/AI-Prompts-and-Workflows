# Service Offer Prompt

## Goal
Turn a rough service idea into a clearer, more structured and more sellable offer.

## Prompt structure
Act as a strategic offer design assistant.

Take the following service idea and transform it into a clearer offer structure.

Organize the output into:
- service name
- target client
- main problem solved
- deliverables
- optional extras
- simple pricing logic
- short positioning summary

Keep the result practical, easy to understand and suitable for a landing page or sales message.

## Use case
Useful for freelancers, service businesses and small digital offers that need clearer packaging and presentation.
